ShamwariPay

ShamwariPay is a payments and money transfer system hosted on the Shamwari betaChain. Payments and transfers are secured settled and recorded through the core infrastracture of the blockchain system and its distributed ledger.

ShamwariPay is hosted on a permissioned betaChain and as such, only approved accounts are able to transact in currencies which themselves do not have a restrictive property.

Due to the system being built on the blockchain, ShamwariPay can support multiple currencies each with its own controls, permissions and taxing properties. The system is designed to support public currencies as well as private-issued service currencies

Transaction Processing

ShamwariPay uses the distributed network of the parent blockchain for transaction initialization, execution and confirmation. This mechanism results in cheaper setup costs for businesses which integrate the system into their operations and lower transaction charges and fees for customers.

Execution

Transactions on ShamwariPay can only be executed from a Munhumutapa account to another account which is the recipient. Each transactionis firstchecked fo validity in relation to the currency controls and taxation.

Broadcast

Once a transaction is initialised, it is broadcast to the blockchain network for distribution to each node which rechecks the transaction for validity.

Confirmation

Valid transactions are subsequently confirmed and added to the ledger and signed after which it is part of the blockchain where they can be publicly viewed, verified and referenced.

Applications
Payments

The system can be integrated by businesses as a payment option for their customers. Online payments can also be integrated with online-shopping websites and platforms.

Currency Transfers

ShamwariPay also supports money transfers between any permissioned accounts including peer-to-peer transfers and remittances and online mobile transfers.

Transaction Fees

Only transaction fees are charged on ShamwariPay. There are no other fees paid on the system other than these i.e no monthly, withdrawal, deposit fees etc.

Transactions on ShamwariPay are subject to transaction fees but are however not paid in the currency involved in the transaction but rather in the utility coin of the host chain's betaCoin.

Fees on ShamwariPay are not determined by the transaction amount but are instead set at a pre-determined minimum fee. Fee variations are however present in times where there are very high transaction volumes in which case, the higher the fee paid for a transaction, the faster it is processed.
