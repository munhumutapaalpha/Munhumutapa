Munhumutapa For Business

The Munhumutapa Blockchain can be integrated into your business to digitise operations and transactions within the organizations as well as with your customers. Given the size of the platform, there are inumerable ways you can make Munhumutapa part of your business and how you do it us dependent on your needs.

Sales

Munhumutapa Blockchain systems can power your sales through private tokens/currencies that you can issue to your customers for use in payments on your product or service. This means you can now turn coupons into digital currencies that are fungible, transferable and decimalised. The blockchain can also give your business the ability to implement a prepaid business model which can benefit your operations.

Subscriptions

By making use of either Munhumutapa's digital asset system or currency system, your business can issue a custom digital subscription medium which can be applicable only to your provided service. Through these systems, as a business you are in total control of supply, tradeability, value and use of your subscriptions thereby protecting your business and your customers from externally-induced depreciations and/or inflations.

Rewards

The currency and digital asset systems can also be used in your business' rewards programs, be it rewards based on sales or loyalty. By running your rewards program in Munhumutapa, your business enjoys the benefits which include: 1. Cheap program launch fees. 2. Low program run and maintenance costs. 3. Easy and fast integration of programs into your business, 4. Access of programs across borders. Using these systems, as a business, you can also track usage, engagement, popularity and distributions of your rewards programs.

Promotions

Munhumutapa can also be used to manage promotions conducted by your business through the various systems in the platform. This could be achieved through collections of tokens, NFTs, random picking based on digital balances etc.

Employee Identification

Accounts on the platform can be assigned properties by other accounts depending in their use case. This then opens up a system of identifying your employees, their posistions, etc by your business but also by the public which will be exposed to your team structure.

Access and Authorization

Munhumutapa accounts are secured by 12+ word passphrases and these accounts can be used as access tools to various parts of your business systems or premises. An in-built multi-signatory system also allows a distributed management system to be implemented in your organization.

Customer Database

Since Munhumutapa is a distributed ledger system, the platform by default keeps all data recorded on it thus making it a database on which your business can track its business transactions as well as trends, customers, etc.
