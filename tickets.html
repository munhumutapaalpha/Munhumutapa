Munhumutapa Ticketing System

The Munhumumutapa Ticketing system is a distributed app on which users can list and sell tickets for events directly to other users cia the blockchain. The system is designed with a payments facility as well as sales tracking and a refunds processor. The system also works as an events board where users can look up lined up events in their area or generally accessible to them.

Features

Events Listing
Tickets Listings & Sales
Tickets Purchases
Ticket Purchases Verification
Tickets Purchases Refunds
Tickets Discounts
Events & Ticketing History
Ticket Sales Tracking

Advantages of Munhumutapa Ticketing System

Direct payments to event host on sales
Funds Debit After Event Confirmation
One Transaction Refunds
Paperless proof-of-purchase
Discount Functionality
