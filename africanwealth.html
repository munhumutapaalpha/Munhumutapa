AfricanWealth BetaChain

The AfricanWealth betaChain is the main betaChain of the Munhumutapa platform. The betaChain carries all the universal distributed applications built into Munhumutapa. Universal distributed applications are different in that they can only be accessed and used on the AfricanWealth betaChain and are not portable to other chains. As the main betaChain, AfricanWealth is thus the economic hub of the Munhumutapa Alphablockchain platform.

WEALTH(WEALTH) BetaCoin

Transactions on the AfricanWealth chain are facilitated by its native coin, WEALTH. The coin is a utility medium for access and use of the betaChain's distributed apps.
