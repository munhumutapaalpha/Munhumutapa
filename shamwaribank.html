Shamwari Bank

Shamwari BetaChain

The Shamwari betaChain hosts the monetary and currency system of the Munhumutapa platform. The chain powers a digital bank which allows for the deposits and withdrawals of digital currencies issued. As a result of its application, the Shamwari betaChain is permissioned, i.e. Only users granted access to the chain by its adminstrators can transact on Shamwari betaChain.

Shamwari BetaCoin(MARI)

The utility coin of the Shamwari betaChain is the MARI coin and is the only accepted coin for all transactions initiated on the betaChain. The MARI coin is however not exchangable via the Munhumutapa Exchange Centre or transferable to accounts not granted permission to transact on the chain.

QouteToPayment
